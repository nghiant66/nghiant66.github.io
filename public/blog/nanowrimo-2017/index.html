    Trying NaNoWriMo Again!

    Nghia Ngo

    2017-11-01 14:08

    Hi! So I am trying my hand at NaNoWriMo again this year! I am officially participating in NaNoWriMo 2017, or "National Novel Writing Month." This means, by November 30, 2017, I will have written a 50,000-word novel.

    Last year I got to just around 10,000 words, and then Drumpf was elected and I couldn't concentrate on writing. My goal this year is to at least get halfway done (25,000 words) and have an actual written plan for my novel in advance (which I did complete!).

    If you want to follow my NaNoWriMo progress, here are a few links to check out:

    • NaNoWriMo Official Profile
    • NaNoWriMo Official Novel Page
    • GitLab repo housing my novel
    • My tweets about NaNoWriMo

    Thanks! See you in 50,000 words!
